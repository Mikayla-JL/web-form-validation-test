Browser

Look up the version number of your browser.

For example, on Chrome, the version number might be 11.0.9600.17631. Just enter the first two digits - 11.

How to find your browser version number

Mobile phone or tablet? Type in the name of your phone or tablet

Internet Explorer & Firefox: on the menu bar, click Help and then About Internet Explorer.

It may open in a new tab.

Chrome: click the 'hamburger' icon (3 horizontal lines, on far right of your browser address bar), then About Google Chrome.

If you are not running the most recent version of Chrome, it will ask you to update. Don't click OK, we do not want you to update during the session.

Safari: on the menu bar, click Safari, then About Safari.
